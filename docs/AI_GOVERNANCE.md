# AI governance

Material recommendations require customer, message, transaction or approved-document sources. The UI distinguishes source evidence, deterministic calculation and AVO inference; shows confidence and uncertainty; and warns staff to verify before approval. Application logic rechecks price, promotion dates, availability, consent and policy conflicts.

All AVO work stays Draft. Original output and the human-edited version are retained. Useful/partial/not-useful/incorrect feedback is supported; incorrect requires a correction note. AVO cannot approve or execute. Data minimisation masks unnecessary identifiers and the active provider is disclosed. No hidden-reasoning claims or regulatory certification are made.
