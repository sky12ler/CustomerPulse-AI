# Privacy

CustomerPulse AI offers privacy-supporting governance features and does not claim universal legal compliance or certification. Organisations must configure lawful purposes, retention, notices and access for their jurisdiction. Marketing requires recorded consent; withdrawn contacts are excluded. External AI processing is disclosed and minimised, while demo mode sends nothing to OpenAI.

Use synthetic data until organisational security, DPA, key management, incident response, backup, recovery and deletion procedures have been independently reviewed.
